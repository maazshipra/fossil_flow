Flowman Metal

Fossil Flow is the flagship brand of Flowman Metal Industries, a Pune-based manufacturer and wholesaler specialising in high-quality CP fittings, bathroom accessories, and sanitary wares.


From basin mixers to wall mixers and overhead showers, every product is engineered for precision, durability, and a flawless chrome finish — delivered directly to contractors, retailers, and project developers at competitive wholesale prices.
