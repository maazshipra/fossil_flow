About Company

Over 20 Years of
Trusted Excellence

Delivering premium brass bathroom solutions since 2004.

Established in 2004, Flowman and Fossil Flow have become trusted names in the CP fittings industry. From our beginnings in Bhiwandi, Maharashtra, we have built a strong wholesale network serving dealers, retailers, builders, and project customers across 20 districts of Maharashtra.

We offer a complete range of CP fittings, faucets, bathroom accessories, sanitary ware, showers, sinks, flush tanks, and related products. Fossil Flow represents our premium collection, while Flowman delivers dependable commercial solutions—both manufactured primarily from premium brass for lasting quality and performance.

📍

Now Serving from Pisoli, Pune

We are proud to expand our presence with a new branch in Pisoli, Pune, stocking our complete range of CP fittings, faucets, sanitary ware, showers, sinks, flush tanks, and bathroom accessories for faster delivery and better service across Maharashtra.
